What is anime? Japanese film and or animation can be called anime, but you probaly already knew that if you are here. For those of you who are not as familar with anime, most people compare it with Avatar: The Last Airbender because the style are the same, however; Avatar is not consider an anime due to being made in the U.S. Most anime are based on their respective manga conterparts, kind of like how the Harry Potter movies are. Manga is Manga are comics or graphic novels originating from Japan. We are going to cover my recommendation for some anime based on what I have watched as well as one that are very highly rated on site like MAL, IMDb, and ANiList. We will also do this for both Manga Suggestion, which I will include Manhwa, which is not the same as manga because it they are made in South Korea. Most people just lump these to into the same category anyways. Finally, we will dive into the best opening songs and ending songs in anime.

Below is one of my favorite quotes from an anime is from from One Piece
「一番上に立つ人が何が悪いのか、何が正しいのかを決定します！この場所は中立的な立場です！正義が勝つでしょう、とあなたは言いますか？しかしもちろんそうです！この戦争に勝った人は誰でも正義になります！」 〜ドンキホーテドフラミンゴ（ワンピース）
The translation of this is --- "Those who stand at the top determine what's wrong and what's right! This very place is neutral ground! Justice will prevail, you say? But of course it will! Whoever wins this war becomes justice!"~ Don Quixote Doflamingo (One Piece)
